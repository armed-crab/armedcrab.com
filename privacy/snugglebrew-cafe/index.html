Privacy Policy

Snugglebrew Café

Effective date: April 19, 2026

This policy describes what data Snugglebrew Café collects, how that data is used, and your rights as a player. If anything here is unclear or you'd like to exercise a right described below, email us at armedcrab@gmail.com.

Who we are

Snugglebrew Café is made by Armed Crab, a solo game studio based in the United States. Our website is armedcrab.com. For privacy questions about this game or any other Armed Crab title, contact us at armedcrab@gmail.com.

What Snugglebrew Café collects

Snugglebrew Café stores the following on your device only:

  • Your cafe's progression (days played, customers served, equipment purchased, decorations placed).
  • Your character arcs and story progress (which character visits you've seen, which narrative beats you've unlocked).
  • Your journal / cafe log entries (kept together with progression — these are local narrative keepsakes, not notes sent anywhere).
  • Game settings (audio, accessibility, control preferences).
  • A small amount of UI state (which tutorial prompts you've dismissed).

This data is stored by the app using standard device storage (localStorage and IndexedDB inside the game's WebView). None of it is transmitted to Armed Crab or to any Armed Crab server. Armed Crab does not operate a backend, an account system, or any player database. We have no way to identify an individual player.

Clearing the app's storage (through your device's settings, or by uninstalling the app) removes all of this data. There is no cloud backup — if you uninstall, your cafe is gone.

Snugglebrew Café does not collect:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or contact list
  • Your precise location
  • Your photos, camera feed, microphone, or any media
  • Any payment information (the game has no in-app purchases)
  • Any account credentials (there are no accounts)

Third-party services

Snugglebrew Café does not currently integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. The game is fully standalone — no ads, no analytics, no crash-reporting sent off-device.

If this changes in a future update (for example, if we add AdMob rewarded ads in the same model we use for other Armed Crab games), we will update this policy with the full disclosure before the update ships, and the "Changes to this policy" section will reflect the effective date of that change.

Cookies and local storage

Snugglebrew Café is built on web technology (HTML5) and runs inside a native app container. It uses localStorage and IndexedDB inside the app to persist save data on your device. It does not use cookies in the browser sense, and it does not participate in any cross-site tracking.

Data sharing

Armed Crab does not sell, rent, or share player data. We have no player database to share from, and no third-party SDKs in the current build to share through.

Children's privacy

Snugglebrew Café is a general-audience game and is not directed at children under 13. Armed Crab does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your under-13 child has been using Snugglebrew Café and that personal information was collected, email us at armedcrab@gmail.com and we will delete any information we may have. (As noted above, the game stores data on the device only — clearing app data removes everything.)

Your rights

If you're in the EU, EEA, or UK (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access information about what's collected
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your data ("right to erasure")
  • Object to processing
  • Port your data to another service

Because everything Snugglebrew Café stores lives on your device, you can exercise access, correction, and deletion rights directly: check your device's app storage settings, clear app data, or uninstall.

If you're in California (CCPA / CPRA)

You have the right to:

  • Know what categories of personal information are collected (listed above)
  • Delete personal information (see local-device note above)
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information — we don't sell your data
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising a privacy right

How to exercise a right

Email armedcrab@gmail.com. We'll respond within 30 days.

Security

The save data Snugglebrew Café stores lives on your device, protected by your device's operating system. No system is perfectly secure, but we commit to taking reasonable, practical measures appropriate to a small studio with no player database and no third-party SDKs.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we'll change the effective date at the top and note the change in the game's next release notes on the Play Store. Material changes (for example, adding a new third-party SDK) will be surfaced in-game where practical. Continued use of Snugglebrew Café after changes means you accept the updated policy.
