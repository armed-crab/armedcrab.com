Void Remnant

Last updated: April 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Armed Crab ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in the Void Remnant mobile application.

Information we collect

We do not collect any personal information directly. We do not require account creation, login, or any user registration.

Advertising

Void Remnant uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized ads, including device identifiers and usage data. For more information on how Google uses this data, please visit policies.google.com/privacy.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting google.com/settings/ads.

Data storage

Void Remnant stores your best score locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Children

Void Remnant is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at armedcrab@gmail.com.
