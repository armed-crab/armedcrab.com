About
Games worth playing.

Armed Crab is an independent game studio with a straightforward mission: make games with tight mechanics, genuine replayability, and worlds that feel alive. We span genres — from dark roguelites to cozy sims — because great game design isn't tied to one box.

The philosophy

Ship something real, then make it better. Every game we release is a focused experience — no filler, no padding. Just mechanics that hold up and worlds worth spending time in.

What's next

Void Remnant is out now on Android — our first release, and the start of the catalog. More games are in active development. We'll share them when they're ready.
